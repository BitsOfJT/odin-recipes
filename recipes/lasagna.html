Description

Best ai lasagna you've ever ate with your eyes. It's not actually real. Just an image.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • beef
  • flour
  • TLC

Steps

  1. Make flour into pasta layers.
  2. Make tomatoes into sauce
  3. Layer pasta, beef, sauce, cheese in that order. Do this as many times as you want.
  4. Pay attention not to burn it!
