Old Fashioned Recipe

Home

Description

Best ai old fashioned you've ever drank with your eyes. It's not actually real. Just an image.

Ingredients

  • bourbon
  • sugar cube
  • bitters
  • ice
  • TLC

Steps

  1. Muddle sugar cube and bitters together to a paste.
  2. Pour 2oz bourbon into mixer with paste.
  3. Add ice and stir for about 10 sec.
  4. Strain into drinking glass over fresh balled ice.
