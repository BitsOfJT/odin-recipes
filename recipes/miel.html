Cafe Miel Recipe

Home

Description

Best ai cafe miel you've ever tasted with your eyes. It's not actually real. Just an image.

Ingredients

  • espresso
  • honey
  • cinnamon
  • whole milk
  • TLC

Steps

  1. Pull a double shot into glass with honey in it.
  2. Stir to melt the honey and blend with espresso.
  3. Steam milk into it is hot and silky.
  4. Pour espresso in drinking mug, add cinnamon, then make your latte art.
